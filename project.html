Our Projects

Faisalabad Grid Station

Location: Faisalabad, Punjab
Client: WAPDA
Scope: Complete upgrade of 132kV substation including transformer installation, HT switchgear, and protection panels.
Status: Completed 2024
Highlights: Maan Electric Care executed this project under a tight deadline, ensuring minimal downtime for the regional grid. The work involved replacing two 25 MVA transformers and installing modern numerical relays, enhancing grid reliability.

Sundar Industrial Estate

Location: Lahore, Punjab
Client: Sundar Industrial Estate Management
Scope: Design and installation of 11kV distribution panels, underground cabling, and factory electrification for 15 industrial units.
Status: Ongoing (expected completion Q3 2025)
Highlights: This project requires coordination with multiple factories to avoid production stoppages. MEC’s team is implementing a phased approach, ensuring uninterrupted power supply during installation.

Gujranwala Grid Transformer Replacement

Location: Gujranwala, Punjab
Client: WAPDA / NTDC
Scope: Replacement of two aging 25 MVA power transformers, including associated civil works, busbar modifications, and commissioning.
Status: Completed 2023
Highlights: The project was completed ahead of schedule, restoring full capacity to the grid during peak summer months. MEC’s meticulous planning ensured zero safety incidents.

LDA Avenue Residential Complex

Location: Lahore, Punjab
Client: Lahore Development Authority (LDA)
Scope: Complete electrical infrastructure for 500 residential units, including MV/LV distribution, street lighting, and metering.
Status: Completed 2024
Highlights: MEC coordinated with multiple contractors to synchronise electrical installation with civil works. The project received safety commendation from LDA.

Steel Mill Underground Cabling

Location: Karachi, Sindh
Client: Agha Steel Industries
Scope: Supply and installation of 11kV underground cabling (XLPE, 300 sq.mm) between main grid and mill substations, including termination and testing.
Status: Ongoing
Highlights: The project involves trenching through an active industrial area. MEC’s HSE plan ensures worker safety and minimal disruption to mill operations.
